How to Delete Your Naqlee Account

If you wish to delete your account and associated data, follow these steps:

Steps to Account Deletion

  1. Log in to your Naqlee Account.
  2. Go to Dashboard Page.
  3. Click on Delete Account and confirm your request.

What Data is Deleted?

Once your account is deleted, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number, profile).
  • Booking history.
  • Payment details.
